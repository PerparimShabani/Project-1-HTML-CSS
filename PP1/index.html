Flexibility and Strength:
Regular practice of yoga postures (asanas) improves flexibility and helps to increase muscle strength,
tone, balance, and overall strength. The various poses (asanas) and movements in yoga target different muscle groups, leading to increased flexibility and strength throughout the body.

Balance and Stability:
Yoga poses often involve balancing postures that enhance stability and improve overall balance.
This is particularly beneficial for older adults in preventing falls and maintaining coordination.

Body Awareness:
Yoga encourages mindfulness and body awareness. Through the practice of asanas and focused breathing, individuals develop a deeper connection with their bodies, leading to an increased ability to recognize and address physical imbalances or discomfort

Posture and Alignment:
Yoga helps improve posture and alignment by strengthening the core muscles that support the spine. Proper alignment not only enhances physical appearance but also reduces the risk of back, neck, and other musculoskeletal problems.
